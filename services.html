Services

Family Dentistry

Examinations

Assessing your oral hygiene to check for cavities, gum inflammation, and other signs of gum disease.

X-rays

Imaging of your teeth from multiple angles to understand your oral health and check for oral or gum diseases.

Teeth Cleaning

Removal of plaque and tartar buildup, teeth polishing, and flossing in hard-to-reach places.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Porcelain Veneers

Improving the shape of your smile through fixing crowding or excess space between your teeth.

Ceramic/Gold Crowns

Improving the shape of your smile after instances of tooth decay or root canal therapy. Metal-free options available.

Teeth Whitening

A safe and reliable way for restoring your bright smile and reducing stains or yellowing.

Dental Repair

Fixed Bridges

Using existing teeth to bridge the gap of a missing tooth. Fixed bridges are available in gold or ceramic.

Dentures

Replacing missing teeth and tissue using a removable full teeth replacement.

Removable Partial Dentures

Replacing missing teeth and tissue using a removable partial teeth replacement.

Root Canals

Repairing natural teeth that have been infected, damaged, or are decaying.

Composite Fillings

Filling cavities or fixing disfigured teeth due to decay. Composite fillings are tooth-colored and metal-free.

Amalgam Fillings

Filling cavities or fixing disfigured teeth due to decay. Amalgam fillings are silver-colored and use a metallic mixture.

Implant Restorations

Restoring damaged implants that replaced missing or infected teeth.

Tooth Extraction

Remove a teeth due to severe infection, decay, damage, or crowding.

Nitrous Oxide

A safe and effective option for reducing pain and anxiety during dental procedures.
